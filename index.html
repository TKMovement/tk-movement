About

TK Movement is the personal training and movement coaching brand of Dan Brighurst, created to support people who need a calmer, more thoughtful approach to exercise.

Dan’s coaching approach combines strength training, functional movement, and martial arts-based body awareness. It is designed for people who want to improve mobility, rebuild strength after injury, and feel more capable in everyday life.

The service is especially suited to those who have felt excluded by mainstream fitness culture or who need a more individualised starting point.

Dan is completing a NZQA Level 4 Certificate in Exercise, has a NZQA Level 3 Certificate in Coaching, and is a martial arts instructor and founder of Taikan Kai Karate.

Sessions aim to reduce fear around movement and replace it with structure, clarity, and gradual progress.

TK Movement is guided by Taikan Kai principles while operating as an independent personal training service.

Approach

Sessions are designed to be respectful, clear, and responsive to the individual. The emphasis is on coaching people, not just prescribing exercises.

Respectful and inclusive

  • Communication is adapted to the individual
  • Clients are treated with dignity and respect
  • Boundaries, comfort, and personal context matter

Within scope

  • Training stays within exercise professional scope
  • Medical or injury concerns are referred when needed
  • Recent or unresolved injuries may require clearance from a GP or physiotherapist

Built for real life

  • Focus on long-term function rather than trends
  • Progressions are realistic and sustainable
  • Confidence grows alongside physical capacity
